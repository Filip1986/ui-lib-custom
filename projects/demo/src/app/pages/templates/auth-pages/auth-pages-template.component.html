Overview

Three complete authentication screens — Login, Register, and Forgot Password — all styled with CSS variables so they automatically inherit your brand theme. Keyboard-accessible and screen-reader ready.

Login

Email/password form with "Remember me" and OAuth social buttons.

Register

Name, email, password, and confirm-password with inline validation hints.

Forgot Password

Single-field email form with clear confirmation feedback.
